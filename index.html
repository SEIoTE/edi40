Workshop CfP Invitation

Software Engineering in Industry 4.0 Ecosystems (SEIoTE)

Thessaloniki, Greece (April 22-24, 2025)

About the Workshop

You are cordially invited to participate in the second workshop on Software Engineering in Industry 4.0 Ecosystems, co-located with the 8th International Conference on Emerging Data and Industry (EDI40). The primary theme for this year is the Maintenance of Software and Data in interconnected Industry 4.0 environments. The workshop will explore how to ensure long-term stability, security, and sustainability of software systems and data assets amidst evolving technologies, including firmware, operating systems, libraries, programming languages, databases, and AI-driven processes.

Main Themes:

  • Software and Data Maintenance in Ecosystems
  • Architectural and Security Constraints
  • Testing, Verification, and Continuous Integration
  • Managing Dependencies, Updates, and Release Cycles
  • AI in Software and Data Maintenance
  • Open Source vs Proprietary Systems
  • Regulations and Standards Compliance
  • Sustainability of Software and Data
