SEIoTE 2025 Workshop

Software Engineering in Industry 4.0 Ecosystems

Patras, Greece (April 22-24, 2025)

About the Workshop

You are cordially invited to participate in the second workshop on Software Engineering in Industry 4.0 Ecosystems, co-located with the 8th International Conference on Emerging Data and Industry (EDI40). This year, the theme focuses on Maintenance of Software and Data in interconnected Industry 4.0 environments. The workshop will explore long-term stability, security, and sustainability of software systems and data assets amidst evolving technologies, such as firmware, operating systems, libraries, programming languages, databases, and AI-driven processes.

Main Themes

  • Maintenance strategies for software and data in Industry 4.0 ecosystems
  • Automated testing, continuous integration, and deployment in maintenance workflows
  • Managing software dependencies, updates, and versioning in interconnected systems
  • Security challenges in maintaining critical software and data
  • Sustainability in software and data management for the future of Industry 4.0
  • AI-driven maintenance solutions for proactive and predictive problem solving
